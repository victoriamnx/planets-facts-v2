Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun. Its
name is a reference to the Greek god of the
sky, Uranus according to Greek mythology,
was the great-grandfather of Ares. It has
the third-largest planetary radius and
fourth-largest planetary mass in the Solar System.
